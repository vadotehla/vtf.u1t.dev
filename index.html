What we do

We are an independent, nonincorporated organization dedicated to advancing computation and programming. We develop and maintain projects like DevKit (a schema‐based webpage rendering engine based on WebGPU and native graphical libraries), DotADX (a custom browser and intranet solution), and various bytecode and binary‐like languages.
Our mission is to create high‐quality, free solutions and to better the global technology.
